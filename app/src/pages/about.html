About Uttarakhand Midwest Association

Uttarakhand Midwest Association(UMA) was formed in 2003. Its core vision is to strive for preserving cultural heritage of Uttarakhand and to spread awareness by inculcating the value system of togetherness, sharing, caring and good-will in our future generation. UMA proudly welcomes all having roots attached to Uttarakhand state in India. UMA is committed to promote the ethnic heritage of Uttarakhand, by hosting cultural events throughout the year by bringing the families and individuals together under one platform and share their values and experiences . UMA firmly believes that we all are fortunate enough to be a part of the legacy of our Devboomi, and we all must assume our responsibility to extend these values in our forthcoming generation. In this fast paced world, where we all continuously looking to move ahead of time, where we all looking to better everyone, UMA has its aim to protect and preserve the core values of our culture through collaboration, peace and mutual co-operation.
